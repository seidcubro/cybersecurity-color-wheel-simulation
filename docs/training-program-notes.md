# Training Program Notes

This simulation can be adapted into a corporate cybersecurity training exercise.

## Why it works well for training

It teaches participants how to:
- handle incomplete evidence
- communicate across technical and non-technical stakeholders
- justify containment decisions
- balance operational continuity against security risk
- document a full investigation professionally

## Enterprise training use cases

- SOC analyst tabletop exercise
- IAM and MFA abuse response drill
- Incident commander training
- Cross-functional security collaboration exercise
- Security awareness follow-up after phishing or MFA fatigue incidents

## Suggested progression

### Level 1
Identity compromise and MFA fatigue only

### Level 2
Identity compromise plus VPN access and limited lateral movement

### Level 3
Full end-to-end attack chain with data staging and exfiltration indicators

## Suggested deliverables for trainees

- running log
- timeline
- containment plan
- executive summary
- lessons learned and control recommendations
